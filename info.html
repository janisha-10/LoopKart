How to use:

  1. 1. After creating an account, finish setting up your profile and add all relevant information.
  2. 2. For buyers:

    1. (i) On the dashboard, search for required material.
    2. (ii) Scroll through result to find desired material.
    3. (iii) Compare price, seller rating, condition of material etc. to select most appropriate item.
    4. (iv) Select the amount/quantity of items that you wish to buy.
    5. (v) Send the seller a buying request by clicking the add to cart icon.
    6. (vi) Connect with seller through their respective contact details, which will be visible once your request has been approved.
    7. (v) Once the transaction is complete, kindly rate the seller depending on your experience and the product quality.

    8. 3. For sellers:

      1. (i) On the dashboard, click on "add" to enlist an item you wish to sell.
      2. (ii) Enter the required details, along with a picture of the item.
      3. (iii) Approve buyer requests and connect with them to sell you material.
      4. (iv) Be honest about the products you are selling since this will affect your rating, which will impact future transaction.
