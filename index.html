Don't dump it, loop it!

We at loopkart believe every resource should be utilised to its full extent, so we made a platform to facilitate exchange of excess materials. LoopKart is a digital platform in line with SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the goals), where industries list surplus or waste materials, enabling NGOs, startups, and individuals to buy them at a low cost. It promotes reuse of materials, reduces waste, and supports a circular economy.
