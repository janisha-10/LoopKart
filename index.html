LoopKart

Don't dump it, loop it!

In line with SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the goals) We at loopkart believe every resource should be utilised to its full extent, so we made a platform to facilitate exchange of excess materials. LoopKart is a digital platform where industries list surplus or waste materials, enabling NGOs, startups, and individuals to buy them at a low cost. It promotes reuse of materials, reduces waste, and supports a circular economy.

Current Problem:

  • Industries discard usable surplus materials
  • NGOs & small businesses lack affordable raw materials
  • Recyclable items reach landfills too early
  • No simple platform for material reuse collaboration

All of which leads to:

  • Resource waste
  • Higher costs
  • Environmental damage

Key features of LoopKart:

  • Surplus Material Listings
  • Lifecycle Status Tags
  • Location-based matching
  • Rent / Lend option for usable items
  • Impact tracking (waste reused, landfill avoided)
